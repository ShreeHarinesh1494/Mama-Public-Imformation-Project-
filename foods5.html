Best foods for the Second trimester(5 Month)


Dried fruit

Dried fruit such as apricots, dates and figs contain calcium and iron, and are a great snack to stash in your desk at work or the kitchen at home for any easy grab-and-go snack. Or you could add some chopped dried fruit to low-fat yoghurt.


Brown rice vegetable risotto

Wholegrain or brown rice and mushrooms contain magnesium, fibre, vitamin D and calcium. Try a mushroom risotto with cooked spinach on the side, or with peas mixed through. The fibre will also help to prevent pregnancy constipation.


Plain yoghurt

Plain, unsweetened yoghurt is a calcium-rich snack. Keep a pot in the fridge, or take individual portions to work. Try low-fat Greek yoghurt or fromage frais, and add fresh fruit such as blackberries, which contain magnesium.

Peanut butter on wholemeal bread

Peanut butter, or any nut butter, contains healthy fats, and is a good source of magnesium and iron, as is fortified wholegrain bread. The two together make a snack that will fend off any hunger pangs, and a glass of milk on the side adds calcium.


Cheese on seeded bread

Low-fat cream cheese, or any cheese that you like, on seeded bread, will give you calcium as well as magnesium. Have with crunchy salad such as radishes and cucumber.


Hummus

Have a pot of hummus in the fridge for a tasty way to get calcium, magnesium and iron. A small wholegrain pitta or granary bread and crunchy raw vegetables will add magnesium.


NOTE

The foods mentioned above can be taken with others foods also.Which your consulted Doctor has been described to you.There are many foods to be taken and avoided during preganancy.The above mentioning foods ar some of them.
